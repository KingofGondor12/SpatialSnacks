This one is for Chicken Risotto

  • Ingredients
  • Recipe

Ingredients:
- 1 Tablespoon Of Olive Oil
- 1 Brown Onion, Finely Chopped
- 400g Of Diced, Fat-Trimmed Chicken Thigh Fillets
- 2 Garlic Cloves, Crushed
- 250g of SunRice Arborio Risotto Rice
- 125ml of White Wine
- 600ml of Massel Salt Reduced Chicken Style Liquid Stock
- 25g of Grated Parmesan Cheese, Plus 2 Tablespoons To Serve
- 200g Sweet Corn Kernels
- 200g of Halved Grape Tomatoes
- 75g of Baby Spinach Leaves

Recipe:
Step 1 - Heat oil in a large heavy based saucepan over medium-high heat.
Step 2 - Cook onion and chicken stirring occasionally, for 3-4 minutes, or until golden.
Step 3 - Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds.
Step 4 - Add rice and stir to coat grains in oil.
Step 5 - Add wine and simmer for 1 minute until absorbed.
Step 6 - Add stock and bring to the boil.
Step 7 - Cover, reduce heat to low and simmer for 10-12 minutes or until rice is al dente and stock is absorbed.
Step 8 - Stir in cheese, corn, tomatoes and spinach.
Step 9 - Season
Step 10 - Cover and set aside for 2 minutes.
Step 11 - Serve immediately garnished with extra cheese.
